This site is a public notebook. I built it to document the reality of building software. When things break, when a technical choice matters, or when I finally figure a system out, I take notes and leave them here.

There is enough noise in tech right now. I prefer to focus on the code. You will find practical logs here, my current architecture choices, and probably a few sharp opinions.

Lately, I spend my time exploring AI tools to see what actually works beyond the hype. I also dig into NixOS configurations and I am slowly getting into robotics. 

This is raw documentation. No fluff, no extended essays. Just a developer keeping track of the work.
